Product Information

Our products are specifically engineered to accommodate the full spectrum of mustache architectures, from the modest pencil to the majestic handlebar. The Executive Shaper Pro and Elevate X1 Harness are particularly well-suited for handlebar styles, featuring our patented Curl Retention Technology.

For exceptionally wide handlebars (exceeding 4 inches per side), we recommend the XL sizing option for optimal curl support.

Most of our products are available in Small, Medium, Large, and Extra Large. Sizing is based on facial width and mustache density, not body size. Our size guide on each product page provides detailed measurements.

For reference: Small accommodates a philtrum width of 12-14mm, Medium 14-16mm, Large 16-18mm, and Extra Large 18mm and above. When in doubt, we recommend measuring twice before ordering.

All of our shapers and harnesses feature micro-adjustment capabilities. The Executive Shaper Pro, for example, includes a tri-axis adjustment system allowing for precision calibration within 0.5mm tolerances.

This adjustability ensures that as your mustache matures and evolves, your harness can adapt accordingly without requiring replacement.

We use only premium materials selected for their comfort, durability, and mustache compatibility. Our products feature medical-grade silicone, aircraft-aluminum alloy frames, and genuine Italian leather where applicable.

All materials are hypoallergenic and have been tested for prolonged skin contact. We never use plastics or synthetic materials that could irritate delicate facial skin or compromise mustache integrity.

Our Night Guards & Sleepwear collection is specifically designed for overnight use. Products like the DreamGuard Deluxe and SleepSecure Mustache Bonnet have been engineered for maximum comfort during all sleep positions.

We do not recommend wearing our daytime Precision Shapers or Support Systems to bed, as they are calibrated for active, upright use. Overnight use could result in over-shaping or muscle fatigue.

Mustache Care

For optimal results, we recommend consistent daily use. Most of our customers wear their Precision Shapers for 4-6 hours during the day, switching to Night Guards during sleep.

Your mustache is a living organism that responds to consistent training. Irregular use may result in uneven curl development or shape regression. Think of it as a workout routine for your whiskers.

Clean your harness weekly with a soft, damp cloth. For silicone components, a mild soap solution may be used. Leather elements should be conditioned monthly with a quality leather balm.

Avoid harsh chemicals, alcohol-based cleaners, or immersing metallic components in water. Proper maintenance extends product life and ensures hygienic contact with your distinguished facial hair.

Mustache wax is fully compatible with all of our products. In fact, we recommend applying wax before donning your harness, as this creates a protective barrier and enhances shape retention.

For Night Guards, we advise applying wax at least 30 minutes before bed to allow proper setting. Excessive wax buildup on harness surfaces should be cleaned weekly as described above.

Our products are designed to shape and support existing hair growth, not to impede it. The gentle pressure exerted by our harnesses actually promotes healthy follicle activity by increasing blood flow to the upper lip region.

Many customers report enhanced growth and thickness after 6-8 weeks of regular harness use, though individual results may vary based on genetics and commitment to the regimen.
