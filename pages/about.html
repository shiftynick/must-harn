Our Story

A Legacy of Mustache Excellence Since 1987

For over three decades, Mustache Harnesses Co. has been the trusted name in facial hair support and styling solutions. What began as one man's frustration with an unruly handlebar has grown into an industry-leading enterprise serving distinguished gentlemen worldwide.

The Bristleworth Vision

It was a crisp autumn morning in 1987 when our founder, Reginald P. Bristleworth III, woke to find his magnificent handlebar mustache had wilted overnight. The tragedy was immeasurable. His prized whiskers, carefully cultivated over seven years of dedicated grooming, hung limply against his cheeks like deflated dirigibles.

"There must be a better way," Reginald muttered to his reflection that fateful morning. And in that moment of despair, an industry was born.

Working tirelessly in his garage workshop, Reginald developed the first prototype of what would become the Executive Shaper Pro. His neighbors thought he had lost his mind. His wife threatened divorce. But Reginald persevered, driven by an unwavering commitment to mustache dignity.

Within three years, Mustache Harnesses Co. had grown from a one-man operation into a legitimate business with two employees and a modest office above a dry cleaner. Today, we serve customers in 47 countries and remain the world's premier provider of mustache support solutions.

Our Mission

To preserve and protect the dignity of distinguished facial hair through innovative support solutions, unwavering quality standards, and a deep respect for the mustache arts.

Every product we create is designed with one principle in mind: your mustache deserves better. Whether you're a CEO presenting to shareholders or a gentleman enjoying a quiet evening at home, your facial hair should reflect the excellence you embody.

Our team doesn't just work with mustaches. We live and breathe facial hair. Several team members have dedicated their entire academic careers to whisker dynamics.

Integrity

We stand behind every product with our ironclad 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If your mustache isn't happier, neither are we.
